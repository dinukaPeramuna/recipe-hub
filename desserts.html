Dessert Recipes

1. Chocolate Mousse

  • Dark chocolate
  • Heavy cream
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  1. Melt chocolate, whip cream, fold in eggs.
  2. Chill before serving.

2. Fruit Salad

  • Bananas, apples, grapes, pineapple
  • Honey
  • Lime juice
  1. Mix fruits, drizzle honey and lime juice.
  2. Serve chilled.
